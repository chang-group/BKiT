Accurate Free Energy and Kinetics Calculation Library

Introduction

Transient states during ligand-protein binding/unbinding reveal the key atomistic interactions that would affect drug pharmacokinetics. Investigation of transient states can help us to alter drugs' residence time, Kon, Koff etc to achieve desired binding kinetics. Traditional drug design merely focuses on bound-state which is not sufficient to predict drug binding/unbinding kinetic properties and it may lead to failure of drug development. Here, we introduce a python toolkit for transient states identification and binding free energy calculation to better understand ligand-protein interactions during binding/unbinding.
